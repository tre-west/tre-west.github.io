Games of Old

Video games has been a part of my life from my childhood to even now. Although I do not play as often, I enjoy playing games when I can. This post is simply going down memory lane for myself. Growing up with my siblings, we played many games together from N64 to the most modern consoles. It was convient that my eldest brother always played Xbox and my father preferred the Playstation. This along with me having a computer, there was no exception to the games I could play. I am going to work through each console and write up each game I throughly enjoyed playing.
